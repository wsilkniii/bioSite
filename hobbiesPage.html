About Hobbies

Plants are definitely one of Brianna's favorite hobbies/things. She loves taking care of her plants and seeing them grow. Having greenery and nature nearby creates a great environment and makes her proud that she is contributing to this plants life.

Within the about page, it is shown that Brianna wants to build a library in her future home. It's no surprise that she loves to read books. She typically likes to read at least 3 books a week, and that's a slow week. She can read very quickly, and loves the fantasy genre. If it contains mythical creatures, she'll love it.

Brianna loves to draw. Art is a creative outlet for her. She loves using Sharpies as her medium. Brianna's art focuses on lines, and popping colors. Sharpie is perfect as it allows crisp lines, and has plenty of bright colors. Brianna doesn't have as much time to draw, but she loves to do it in her free time.
